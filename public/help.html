Help & FAQ

What is Detective Jai?

Detective Jai is a free AI-powered scam detection tool. It checks phone numbers, messages, and links for scam patterns. It is available on Telegram and on the web.

Is Detective Jai free?

Yes. Forever. No sign-up required. No hidden fees. No ads on the tool itself.

How do I use it?

Type commands like /checknumber 08012345678 or paste a suspicious message. Detective Jai will analyze it instantly and tell you if it is a scam.

Where can I use Detective Jai?

On Telegram (@JoshuaGiwaBot), on this website, and soon on WhatsApp and USSD.

How do I report a scammer?

Use the /report command with the number and reason. For example: /report 08012345678 fake loan scam. Your report helps protect others.

What happens after I report a scammer?

The number is added to our scammer database. It will be flagged when other users check it. T.R.I. may investigate the report.

What if my number was wrongly reported?

Use the /plea command. Example: /plea 08012345678 I am a legitimate business. Your case will be reviewed.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not collect or store your personal information. Your reports are anonymous. We do not share your data with anyone.

What if I have already been scammed?

Stop sending money immediately. Report the number to Detective Jai using /report. Contact your bank. Report to the police. You are not alone.

What languages does Detective Jai support?

English for now. Pidgin, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Fulfulde, Ijaw, Tiv, Kanuri, and more coming.

What commands can I use?

/checknumber 08012345678 — Check a phone number
/checkmsg [message] — Analyze a message
/checklink [url] — Check a link
/report [number] [reason] — Report a scammer
/plea [number] [reason] — Appeal a false report
/help — Show all commands

What is T.R.I.?

T.R.I. (Top Rank Investigations) is a security agency that investigates reports submitted through Detective Jai and CivicVoice.

What is CivicVoice?

CivicVoice is a platform where citizens can report structural flaws, road issues, and community concerns. Detective Jai integrates with CivicVoice for infrastructure reports.
